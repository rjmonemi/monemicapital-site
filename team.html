The Principals

Two generations. One standard.

Monemi Capital is owned and operated by a father-and-son team of engineers. Every decision that matters is made by a principal — and every dollar we manage sits beside our own.

RM

Ryan Monemi

General Partner — Acquisitions & Capital

Ryan leads the firm's investment activity: sourcing, underwriting, capital structuring, and lender relationships. He brings a quantitative, systems-driven approach to acquisition analysis — every assumption modeled, every downside scenario priced before a deal moves forward.

Trained as an electrical engineer and published in IEEE, he treats an apartment building the way an engineer treats a system: measurable inputs, controlled execution, and performance that has to be verified — not assumed.

B.S. Electrical Engineering, Cal Poly Pomona · M.S. Electrical Engineering (cand.), USC Viterbi School of Engineering
SM

Dr. Saeed Monemi

Principal — Operations & Asset Management

Dr. Monemi directs everything that happens on the ground: construction oversight, contractor management, and the physical operation of each property through renovation and stabilization. When a scope is written, he is the one standing in the unit making sure it is delivered.

He spent decades in the utility industry, where large-scale infrastructure demands exacting standards for planning, maintenance, and execution — the same standards he now applies to repositioning multifamily assets.

Ph.D. Electrical Engineering · Decades of utility-industry infrastructure experience
