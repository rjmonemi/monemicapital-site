For Owners & Brokers

A direct, reliable buyer for your property.

We are a principal-operated buyer of small multifamily across Southern California. You deal with the people who make the decisions, get a straight answer quickly, and work with a buyer that closes what it signs.

Who You're Dealing With

The decision-maker, not a gatekeeper.

When you bring us a property, your first conversation is with a principal who can underwrite it and commit to it. There is no committee to clear and no acquisitions analyst standing between you and an answer.

We focus on a narrow segment and a defined footprint, so we already know the comps, the rents, and the operating costs on most of what we see. That lets us give you a real read in days, not weeks, and price from knowledge rather than guesswork.

Why Sell to Us

Three things a seller can count on.

Certainty of Close

We underwrite conservatively and only sign what we intend to complete. When we go under contract, we close on the terms we agreed to.

A Fast, Honest Read

If a property fits, you will know quickly and at a real number. If it does not, we will tell you why rather than tie it up and retrade later.

Discretion

Off-market conversations stay off-market. We can move on a property quietly, without a marketing process, when that serves the seller.

How a Transaction Works

Four steps, no surprises.

01

Introduction

Send the address, unit count and mix, current rents, and your timeline. We confirm fit and come back with questions, not a form letter.

02

Indicative Pricing

We run the property against our comps and give you an honest pricing range and the assumptions behind it, before either side spends real time.

03

Letter of Intent

If the range works, we issue a clear LOI with defined terms and timelines. What we put in writing is what we plan to close on.

04

Diligence & Close

We run a focused diligence process and close on schedule. No manufactured issues, no last-minute retrades.
