FAQ

Questions, answered.

The things owners, brokers, and prospective partners ask us most. If your question isn't here, write to us directly and a principal will answer it.

For Owners & Brokers

Selling or sourcing a property.

What exactly do you buy?

Ten to twenty unit B- and C-class multifamily, generally 1960s through 1990s vintage, with sound structure and curable deferred maintenance. We focus on the Inland Empire and buy selectively in workforce corridors of Los Angeles and Orange counties. The full criteria are on our strategy page and owners page.

How quickly will I hear back?

Every inquiry is read by a principal. Anything inside our buy box gets a substantive answer, typically within two business days, not an automated reply.

Do you work with brokers, and do you protect fees?

Yes. Broker relationships are how most of our best opportunities surface, and we treat them as long-term. We respect representation and pay agreed fees in full. Bring us a property on-market or off and you will get a fast, honest read.

Will you buy off-market?

Often, that is exactly how we prefer to transact. We can move quietly on a property without a public marketing process when that serves the seller, and we keep those conversations confidential.

Are you a reliable closer?

We underwrite conservatively and only sign what we intend to complete. When we go under contract, we close on the terms we agreed to. We do not manufacture issues in diligence to retrade the price.

For Capital Partners

Investing alongside us.

Do the principals invest their own money?

Yes. Our own capital is invested in every deal, beside our partners. We carry the same risk and feel the same outcome.

Are you raising money right now?

We build relationships with prospective partners ahead of specific opportunities rather than under deadline pressure. Any actual offering is made only through definitive documents provided to qualified parties. The right time to start the conversation is before there is a deal on the table.

Who can invest?

Opportunities are offered only to accredited investors and other qualified parties, and only on a fully informed basis through formal offering documents you can review with your own advisors.

What returns do you target?

We do not publish return targets or make performance promises on this website, and nothing here is an offer. Any projections specific to an opportunity are shared privately with qualified parties, alongside the assumptions and risks behind them.

How do you report to partners?

We provide regular, plain-language updates measured against the original underwriting. When results differ from the projection, we explain why rather than smoothing it over.

About the Firm

Who you're dealing with.

Who runs Monemi Capital?

It is a two-generation family firm run by Ryan Monemi and Dr. Saeed Monemi, both engineers by training. You can read more on the team page. Every decision that matters is made by a principal.

Why such a narrow focus?

Concentration is the advantage. By staying inside one segment and a defined footprint, we know the comps, costs, and operations block by block, which sharpens both our underwriting and our speed. We would rather know a few corridors completely than the whole map superficially.

Do you manage the properties yourselves?

We oversee them directly. The people who underwrite a deal are the people responsible for the renovation and the ongoing operation. That is the whole point of staying small and focused.

How do I get in touch?

Use the contact form. It reaches a principal directly, and you can expect a substantive reply, typically within two business days.
