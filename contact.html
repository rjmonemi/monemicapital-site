Contact

Start a conversation.

We welcome inquiries from property owners considering a sale, brokers with off-market opportunities, lenders, and prospective capital partners.

Direct correspondence reaches a principal — not a funnel. Expect a substantive reply, typically within two business days.

What to include

For property submissions: address, unit count and mix, current rents, and your timeline. For capital conversations: a note about who you are and what you're looking to accomplish.

Common Questions

Before you write.

What do you buy?

10–20 unit B- and C-class multifamily, 1960s–1990s vintage, with sound structure and curable deferred maintenance — across our Inland Empire core and select Los Angeles and Orange County corridors. The full criteria are on the strategy page.

How quickly will I hear back?

Every inquiry is read by a principal, and anything inside our buy box gets a substantive answer — typically within two business days, not a form letter.

Do you work with brokers?

Yes — broker relationships are how our best opportunities surface, and we treat them as long-term assets. Bring us anything that fits, on-market or off, and you'll get a fast, honest read.

Are you accepting capital partners?

We build relationships with prospective partners ahead of specific opportunities. Any offering is made only through definitive documents provided to qualified parties — but the conversation starts here.
